To incorporate means to unite or merge something with an entity that already exists, making it part of a larger whole. This verb describes the process of integrating new elements, ideas, or components into an established structure, system, or organization. When you incorporate something, you absorb it into something pre-existing rather than creating something entirely new from scratch. The term implies a seamless blending where the added element becomes an integral part of the existing framework, maintaining continuity while expanding or enhancing what was already there.
