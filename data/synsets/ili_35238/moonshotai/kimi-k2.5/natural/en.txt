To teem or swarm means to be filled with a large number of living organisms or people that are moving about in a noisy, bustling manner. A place that teems with activity is densely populated by creatures or individuals in constant motion, often creating an impression of abundant energy and lively chaos. The term is frequently used to describe locations overrun with insects, animals, or crowds of people engaged in vigorous activity. This concept encompasses the sense of a space being abuzz with life and movement, where the sheer quantity of beings generates a palpable sense of vibrant commotion.
