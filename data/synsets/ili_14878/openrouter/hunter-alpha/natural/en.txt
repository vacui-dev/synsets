Bibliographical (or bibliographic) is an adjective referring to anything pertaining to bibliography—the discipline concerned with the systematic recording, cataloging, and analysis of books and other written publications. In academic and library science contexts, the term is used to describe materials, methods, or activities related to the creation and management of bibliographic references and citations. For example, a bibliographic database stores structured records of published works including author names, titles, and publication details. The adjective derives from the noun bibliography and is closely associated with scholarly research and the documentation of information sources.
