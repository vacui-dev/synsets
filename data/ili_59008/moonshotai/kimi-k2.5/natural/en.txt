A ski lodge is a specialized hotel or accommodation facility located at a ski resort, designed to provide lodging for skiers and winter sports enthusiasts. These establishments typically offer convenient access to ski slopes, equipment rental services, and amenities tailored to cold-weather outdoor activities such as heated storage for ski gear and boot drying rooms. Ski lodges range from rustic cabins to luxury hotels, often featuring communal spaces like fireplaces and lounges where guests can relax after a day on the slopes. Many ski lodges also provide dining facilities, ski instruction services, and transportation to nearby ski lifts, making them central hubs for winter vacation experiences in mountainous snow-covered regions.
