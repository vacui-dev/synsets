A bubble bath is a type of bath in which specially formulated liquid or powder is added to running water to create a mass of fragrant, frothy bubbles. The bubbles form on the surface of the water, creating a layer of foam that covers the bather and the tub. Bubble baths are popular for relaxation and recreation, often scented with essential oils or perfumes to enhance the bathing experience. The cleansing agent in the product reduces the surface tension of water, allowing air to become trapped and form stable bubbles that persist throughout the bath.
